# Evidence Rules

Compass scoring must always show the evidence used.

## Evidence Sources

Initial evidence source labels:

```text
Prices
Financials
Company
News
Events
Knowledge
```

## Required Evidence

Every score category should include:

- Evidence source names
- Used metrics
- Missing data
- Reason text

## Category Evidence

Growth:

- Financials
- Knowledge

Financial Health:

- Financials
- Knowledge

Valuation:

- Company
- Knowledge

Momentum:

- Prices
- Knowledge

News:

- News
- Events
- Knowledge

## Rule

If a score cannot explain why points were added or withheld, the scoring rule should be changed.

Evidence is more important than ranking.
