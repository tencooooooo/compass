# Discovery Engine

Discovery Engine is the research layer that identifies companies worth additional investigation.

It is not a ranking engine and it does not make investment decisions.

## Purpose

Discovery Engine connects:

```text
Market
↓
Sector
↓
Company
```

It uses market context, sector trends, company analysis, scoring, news, events, and Knowledge to surface research candidates.

## Design Philosophy

- Explain why a company is selected
- Show evidence sources
- Show confidence
- Preserve watch points
- Avoid buy or sell language
- Avoid black-box ranking

## Relationship With Growth Hunter

Growth Hunter will be the future growth company screening engine.

Discovery Engine is the foundation for Growth Hunter. It prepares candidate selection logic before building a more specialized screening engine.

## Output

Discovery output should include:

- Company
- Discovery Score
- Discovery Reasons
- Strengths
- Watch Points
- Confidence
- Evidence
