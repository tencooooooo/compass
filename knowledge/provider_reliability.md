# Provider Reliability

Compass separates providers by evidence strength.

- Primary information: SEC filings and earnings transcripts.
- Market data: prices, company profiles, and financials from configured providers.
- Secondary information: news, analyst data, and public commentary.
- Derived information: events and normalized signals generated from raw data.
- Estimated information: trends, sentiment, and future experimental providers.

Reliability is one component of Data Quality Score. Primary information receives the highest reliability score, but it still requires freshness and completeness checks.
