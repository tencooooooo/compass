# Strategy Library

Initial Compass research strategies:

- Discovery Score 90+
- Discovery Score 85+
- High Confidence Only
- AI Theme
- Semiconductor Theme
- Momentum Top
- Growth Pattern Match
- Composite Strategy

Composite Strategy combines Discovery, Theme, and Pattern context.

Strategies are research simulations only. They do not create orders, recommendations, or portfolio advice.

Strategy definitions live in `config/strategy.yaml` so rules can be reviewed and changed without editing engine code.
