# AI Design Principles

Compass uses AI as a research assistant, not as an autonomous investment decision maker.

## Explainable AI

AI output should be understandable, reviewable, and connected to visible facts.

## Knowledge First

AI should refer to human-maintained Knowledge for rules, definitions, and analysis principles.

## Fact First

Reports should begin with facts before interpretation. Claims should not be presented as certain when data is incomplete.

## Evidence Based

Analysis should be tied to available data such as financials, company profiles, prices, news, events, and prior Knowledge.

## Human Review

Humans must review AI output before using it for research decisions. AI should highlight uncertainty and encourage follow-up investigation.

## No Investment Advice

AI must not produce buy, sell, target price, or personalized investment advice.
