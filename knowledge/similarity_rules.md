# Similarity Rules

Similarity compares companies using explainable features.

## Feature Groups

- Sector and industry
- R&D intensity
- Profitability
- Valuation
- Momentum
- News and event density
- Validation outcomes
- Market context

## Output

Similarity output must include:

- Similar company
- Similarity score
- Similarity reasons

## Rule

Similarity is not a recommendation. It is a research navigation aid.
