# Time Machine

Compass Time Machine is a Historical Replay engine.

## Purpose

- Recreate what Compass could have known at a specific past date.
- Run historical Discovery, Scoring, and Market summaries without future data.
- Support research into whether Compass would have surfaced useful candidates at that time.

## Rule

Time Machine is not prediction. It is a replay of past available information.

## Output

```text
reports/timemachine/snapshot_YYYY-MM-DD.md
reports/timemachine/discovery_YYYY-MM-DD.md
reports/timemachine/market_YYYY-MM-DD.md
```
