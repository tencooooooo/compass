# Theme Classification

Theme classification combines:

- Company profile
- Sector and industry
- Business summary
- News titles and summaries
- Discovery signals
- Market Intelligence
- Pattern Intelligence
- Memory

Classification rules:

- A company may belong to multiple themes.
- Keyword matches create initial evidence.
- Sector alignment increases confidence but is not sufficient alone.
- News evidence should support the theme when available.
- Theme confidence should be Low, Medium, or High.
- Theme output is a research aid, not an automatic Knowledge update.

When evidence is weak, Compass should keep the classification visible but mark it as Low confidence.
