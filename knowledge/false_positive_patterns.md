# False Positive Patterns

Discovery Engine should track patterns that may look attractive but lead to poor research candidates.

Initial patterns:

- Temporary news spike
- Bubble-like price action
- Excessive expectations
- Material already priced in
- Earnings beat followed by weak reaction
- High momentum without financial support
- High score with low confidence
- One-time event impact
- Low valuation caused by structural decline
- Sector hype without company-specific evidence

## Rule

If a candidate is selected mainly because of one temporary signal, it should be treated carefully.

Discovery should prefer repeatable evidence across market, sector, company, financials, momentum, news, and events.
