# Investment Philosophy

Compass is built for long-term company research.

## Long-term Investing

The project focuses on companies that may compound value over time. Short-term price movement is considered context, not the main objective.

## Company Value

Research should emphasize business quality, competitive position, financial durability, innovation, and management execution.

## Growth

Growth should be evaluated through revenue, earnings, cash flow, reinvestment, market expansion, and product strength. Growth without profitability or durability should be treated carefully.

## Market Psychology

The market often reacts to expectations before facts are fully visible. News, events, volume, and price reaction should be used to understand sentiment and positioning.

## Human Judgment

AI can organize data and identify research questions, but humans must decide whether a company fits their own objectives, risk tolerance, and time horizon.
