# Growth Signals

This file accumulates signals that may appear in future growth companies.

Initial signals:

- Revenue growth
- EPS improvement
- Operating margin improvement
- Free cash flow growth
- Research and development increase
- Market tailwind
- Strong demand
- Product cycle
- Platform shift
- Sector rotation
- Positive event reaction
- Sustained volume increase

These are research signals, not investment conclusions.

Future versions should compare signals against historical outcomes and false positive patterns.
