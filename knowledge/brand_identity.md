# Brand Identity

## Brand Name

Compass

## Subtitle

Explainable AI Investment Research Platform

## Logo Concept

```text
Compass
↓
方向を示す
↓
最終判断は人間
```

Compass is a symbol of direction. It helps humans understand where to look next, but it does not decide the destination.

## Brand Message

```text
答えを出すAIではなく、
考える方向を示すAI。
```

## Future Brand Structure

```text
Compass

Research Engine

Growth Hunter

Scoring Engine

Learning Engine

Dashboard

API
```

## Naming Rules

- Compass is the project brand.
- Growth Hunter is reserved for the future growth stock screening engine.
- Research Engine refers to company analysis.
- Scoring Engine refers to scoring and ranking.
- Learning Engine refers to AI improvement.
- Portfolio Engine may be added later for portfolio research support.
- Dashboard and API may be added later as product interfaces.

## Brand Principle

Compass provides direction, not investment decisions.
