# Workspace Design

Compass Workspace is a daily research workspace, not a marketing dashboard.

## Purpose

- Bring Compass outputs into one readable interface.
- Help the user start each morning with the current research state.
- Keep generated reports visible without hiding uncertainty.
- Preserve the read-only nature of generated research artifacts.

## Structure

- Home
- Discovery
- Company
- Comparison
- Validation
- Proposal
- Learning
- Settings

## Principle

The workspace should reduce the time required to decide what to review next.
