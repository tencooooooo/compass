# Risk Management

Strategy Evaluation should measure risk before celebrating returns.

Core risk metrics:

- Max Drawdown
- Volatility
- Sharpe Ratio
- Position count
- Average Holding Period
- Benchmark Alpha
- Benchmark Beta

Risk rules:

- Empty strategies should be reported as no-position simulations.
- Concentrated portfolios must expose position count.
- Missing benchmark data must not be hidden.
- Strategy results should be compared across multiple periods before any rule is trusted.

Compass does not execute trades. Risk Management here means research transparency and reproducibility.
