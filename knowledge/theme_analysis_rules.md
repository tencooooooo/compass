# Theme Analysis Rules

Theme Intelligence analyzes the chain:

```text
Theme
↓
Market
↓
Sector
↓
Company
```

Each theme report should include:

- Target company count
- Average Score
- Average Discovery score
- Market status
- Representative companies
- Major news
- Validation results
- Momentum
- Confidence
- Similar themes

Theme reports must remain explainable. Every theme assignment should expose the reason it was included.

Theme Intelligence is connected to Discovery because Discovery identifies company candidates inside themes.

Theme Intelligence is connected to Pattern Intelligence because Patterns reveal repeated success, failure, sector, market, and event structures that may strengthen or weaken a theme.

Compass must not automatically change Knowledge based on Theme Intelligence. Human review is required before a theme becomes part of formal Knowledge.
