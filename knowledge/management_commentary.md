# Management Commentary

Management commentary is a qualitative input for long-term research.

Important commentary categories:

- demand environment
- revenue drivers
- margin pressure
- capital allocation
- product roadmap
- customer adoption
- competitive positioning
- supply constraints
- macro sensitivity
- forward guidance

Compass should preserve the original language first. Interpretation, sentiment, and management credibility scoring belong to later analysis layers.

Management commentary can support:

- Company Analysis
- Pattern Intelligence
- Theme Intelligence
- Learning Engine
- Validation review
