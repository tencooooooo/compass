# Historical Analysis

Historical analysis asks:

```text
What would Compass have known on this date?
```

It does not ask:

```text
What happened after this date?
```

## Scope

Historical Replay can use:

- Prices dated on or before the snapshot date
- News dated on or before the snapshot date
- Events dated on or before the snapshot date
- Financial data whose fiscal period ended on or before the snapshot date
- Memory entries dated on or before the snapshot date
- Knowledge versions active on or before the snapshot date

## Difference From Backtesting

Backtesting evaluates what happened after a signal.

Time Machine recreates the signal environment before future outcomes are known.
