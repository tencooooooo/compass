# Theme Library

Compass themes are long-term research lenses that sit above sectors and companies.

Initial themes:

- AI
- Semiconductor
- Cybersecurity
- Cloud
- Robotics
- Healthcare
- Biotechnology
- Energy
- EV
- Space
- Fintech
- Quantum Computing

Theme definitions are maintained in `config/themes.yaml`.

Themes are not exclusive. A company can belong to multiple themes when its business model, news flow, sector context, or Discovery evidence supports more than one classification.

Theme Intelligence does not replace company analysis. It helps humans understand how company-level signals cluster around durable market narratives.
