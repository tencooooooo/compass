# Pattern Confidence

Pattern Confidence describes how reliable a pattern candidate is.

## Levels

```text
High
Medium
Low
```

## Guidance

- High: repeated across several companies and validated periods.
- Medium: supported by multiple signals but limited validation.
- Low: plausible signal with insufficient historical evidence.

## Current Caution

Compass validation history is still limited. Most early patterns should remain Medium or Low until more complete validation periods exist.
