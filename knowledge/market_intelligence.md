# Market Intelligence

Compass is not only a company analysis system.

A company should be understood inside a wider market structure:

```text
Market
↓
Sector
↓
Company
```

## Why Market Intelligence Matters

Company-level data can show financial quality, valuation, momentum, and news reaction. However, companies do not move in isolation.

Market context affects:

- Sector rotation
- Risk-on and risk-off behavior
- News interpretation
- Valuation pressure
- Momentum durability
- Event reaction

## Role In Compass

Market Intelligence Engine organizes:

- Market summary
- Sector composition
- Sector metrics
- Market momentum
- News and event activity
- Rule-based market psychology

It does not generate investment decisions or company rankings.

## Relationship With Growth Hunter

Growth Hunter will be a future growth stock screening engine.

Growth Hunter should run on top of Market Intelligence, because company discovery should consider market and sector context before narrowing down to individual companies.
