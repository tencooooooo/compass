# Relationship Definition

Initial Compass Knowledge Graph relationships:

- `BELONGS_TO`: entity membership in a sector, theme, country, or category.
- `RELATED_TO`: broad contextual relationship from theme, event, or signal overlap.
- `SUPPLIES`: supply-chain relationship or supply-related event.
- `COMPETES_WITH`: same-sector or same-theme company relationship.
- `PARTNERS_WITH`: partnership or alliance relationship.
- `USES`: company use of a technology or product.
- `INVESTS_IN`: investment-related relationship or event.
- `ACQUIRES`: acquisition-related relationship or event.

Every edge should include evidence so Graph consumers can explain why the relationship exists.
