# Branding

## Brand Concept

Compass is an explainable AI investment research platform.

The brand represents direction, not final answers. Compass helps investors organize facts, understand companies, compare candidates, and decide where to investigate next.

## Naming Reason

A compass does not choose the destination for the traveler. It shows direction.

This matches the project philosophy:

- AI should not make investment decisions
- AI should not issue buy or sell calls
- AI should organize evidence and point toward useful research paths
- Humans remain responsible for final judgment

## Future Brand Structure

```text
Compass
    Research Engine
    Growth Hunter
    Scoring Engine
    Learning Engine
    Portfolio Engine
    Dashboard
    API
```

Growth Hunter is reserved for the future growth stock screening engine.

## Logo Image

The logo concept is a compass:

```text
Compass
↓
Shows direction
↓
Final decision remains human
```

The icon should feel calm, precise, and research-oriented rather than speculative or promotional.

## UI Image

The UI should feel like a professional research cockpit:

- Clear navigation
- Dense but readable company data
- Transparent source and evidence sections
- Research priority shown with explanation
- No aggressive trading language
- No casino-like color or motion

Compass should feel trustworthy, explainable, and calm.
