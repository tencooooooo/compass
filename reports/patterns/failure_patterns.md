# Failure Patterns

Pattern candidates require human review before Knowledge updates.

## News-heavy but weak momentum

- Confidence: Low
- Evidence: High news volume with negative 1M momentum may indicate temporary attention.
- Companies: AMD, GOOGL, MSFT, NVDA, TSLA
- Knowledge update: candidate only

## Elevated valuation

- Confidence: Low
- Evidence: Trailing PER above 80 suggests valuation sensitivity.
- Companies: AMD, TSLA
- Knowledge update: candidate only

## Thin profit margin

- Confidence: Medium
- Evidence: Profit margin below 5% can weaken resilience.
- Companies: TSLA
- Knowledge update: candidate only
