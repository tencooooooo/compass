- Confidence: Medium

## Positive EPS and FCF

- Confidence: Medium

- Knowledge update: candidate only

## Sector tailwind

- Confidence: Low
- Evidence: Companies in sectors with positive average 1M momentum.
- Companies: AAPL, AMD, GOOGL, META, MSFT, NVDA
- Knowledge update: candidate only
