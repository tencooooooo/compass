# Compass

Explainable AI Investment Research Platform

## Mission

Compass exists to build AI that understands companies.

It does not try to predict stock prices or replace human judgment. It gathers facts, organizes evidence, explains context, and helps humans decide which companies deserve deeper research.

## Vision

Compass aims to become an explainable AI investment research platform.

The long-term vision is a platform where collectors, Knowledge, analysis, scoring, learning, dashboards, and APIs work together to help investors explore future growth companies with clarity.

Compass provides direction, not answers.

## Core Values

### Knowledge First

Human-maintained Knowledge should guide analysis rules, definitions, assumptions, and project memory.

### Fact First

Reports should begin with observable facts before adding interpretation. Missing data and uncertainty should remain visible.

### Human-in-the-loop

AI supports research, but humans remain responsible for review, interpretation, and final decisions.

### Continuous Learning

The platform should improve through accumulated data, reviewed reports, better Knowledge, and clearer evaluation rules.

### Explainability

Analysis must be understandable. Compass should explain why a company, event, or signal may deserve attention.

## Prohibited Uses

Compass must not:

- Make definitive buy or sell calls
- Provide personalized investment advice
- Present black-box rankings without reasons
- Over-optimize for short-term historical performance
- Hide assumptions, uncertainty, or missing data

## Project Goal

The long-term goal is to build a research platform that helps humans discover future growth companies earlier by organizing facts, patterns, and evidence at a scale humans cannot easily maintain alone.

Compass is a research compass, not an oracle.
